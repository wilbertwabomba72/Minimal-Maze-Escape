Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 6, 2026

Minimal Maze Escape is a single-player offline puzzle game. We do not collect, transmit, sell, or share personal data through the app.

Information Collection

The game does not require an account, login, email address, phone number, location, contacts, photos, microphone, camera, or network access for gameplay.

Local Game Data

The app may store gameplay progress, unlocked levels, star ratings, and audio settings locally on your device. This information stays on your device and is used only to provide the game experience.

Children's Privacy

The app is designed as a general audience puzzle game and does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Third Parties

The app does not include third-party advertising, analytics, tracking SDKs, or external account services.
